Dead Pixels test

This is a simple test designed to make it easier to detect LCD "dead pixels" (pixels that are either stuck-ON or stuck-OFF) by displaying a series of solid background color pages for contrast.
How to test:
1. Make sure your LCD screen is dust free
2. Tap the screen until all 5 colors come up
3. Visually inspect the screen for incorrectly colored pixels
4. Repeat for all 5 colors
